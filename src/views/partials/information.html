Mohit Arora

Creator, Speaker, Advisor

About

Based in Toronto, Canada, and originally from Montreal, I've worked across Canada, USA, UK, and have consulted + spoken in front of audiences in over 20 countries.

Work

Advisor. As an advisor I help startups in consumer tech + health tech connect with their audience and community. I also advise healthcare organizations on issues involving health equity, marketing, and patient engagement. As a rare disease survivor and organ transplant recipient, it is core to my life purpose to ensure my "miracle" outcome becomes the expected outcome for as many people as possible.

Creator. I lead ΛSV Creative Labs, a content studio focused on developing purpose-driven media. After years of developing and nurturing creators, it only made sense to step forward from behind-the-scenes as a creator, myself. I operate across multiple platforms, with a strong focus on live social audio.

Discography. In the past, I've worked in the music industry, ran a social media agency, and consulted for clients in fintech, productivity software, behavioural science, adult entertainment, and so… so much more randomness. For more details you can find me on LinkedIn.

Interests
